Our Story

A Marketplace Born from Craftsmanship

For four decades, IndianHandicraftItems has connected discerning collectors and global buyers with India's most talented artisans. Each object on our shelves is a quiet rebellion against mass production — handmade, individually finished and made to last generations.

From the lattice marble of Agra to the brass foundries of Moradabad, from the carved walnut of Kashmir to the painted scrolls of Odisha — we curate India's living heritage.

Why Choose Us

Craftsmanship You Can Trust

Six promises that have made us the chosen partner for boutique retailers, interior designers and luxury hotels worldwide.

Handmade with Love

Every product is hand-finished by a single artisan — never assembly-line, never automated. Slight imperfections are signatures of authenticity.

Certified Authentic

Each piece carries an authenticity tag with artisan name, region and craft tradition — verifiable provenance for collectors.

Sustainable Sourcing

FSC-certified woods, ethically mined marble, recycled brass. Our supply chain respects the land and the lives behind every craft.

Artisan Crafted

Working directly with 500+ master artisans across 18 Indian states — fair wages, no middlemen, generational livelihoods.

Export Grade Quality

BIS-tested finishes, climate-resistant packaging, customs-cleared documentation — ready for boutiques and hotels worldwide.

Premium Materials

Only the finest sheesham, rosewood, Makrana marble, virgin brass and natural pigments — heirloom-grade by design.
