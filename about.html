Our Mission

Preserving Heritage, One Object at a Time

IndianHandicraftItems began in 1980 in a single workshop in Jaipur — a quiet conviction that India's craft traditions deserved a global stage without losing their soul. Today, we operate from Jaipur, Moradabad and Agra, partnering directly with 500+ master artisans across 18 Indian states.

We don't make products. We carry forward stories — of villages where every household carves rosewood, of families who have inlaid marble for nine generations, of women painting Madhubani by lamplight just as their grandmothers did.

— The IndianHandicraftItems Family
